CHANEL HIGH JEWELRY
SIGNATURE COLLECTIONS

In 1932, Gabrielle Chanel introduced her first and only jewelry collection, BIJOUX DE DIAMANTS, breaking with completely with traditional codes.
The iconic comet, the stones enhanced by the simplicity of their settings,
the word “collection” borrowed from fashion, the use of wax mannequins to display pieces as they would look on the body. This vision of jewelry, this audacity and this unique creativity are still just as present today.
